# NEET 2026 Research Findings (English Medium)

## Syllabus Overview
The NEET 2026 syllabus is based on the NCERT Class 11 and 12 curriculum as finalized by the NMC (National Medical Commission).

## High Weightage Topics

### Biology (50% Weightage - 360 Marks)
- **Human Physiology**: ~20% of the total Biology section. Focus on Digestion, Breathing, Circulation, and Coordination.
- **Genetics and Evolution**: ~18%. High focus on Molecular Basis of Inheritance and Principles of Inheritance.
- **Plant Physiology**: ~15%. Photosynthesis and Respiration are key.
- **Ecology and Environment**: ~12%.
- **Cell Structure and Function**: ~10%.
- **Reproduction**: ~10%.

### Physics (25% Weightage - 180 Marks)
- **Mechanics**: ~30-35%. Units and Measurements, Kinematics, Laws of Motion, Work, Energy, and Power, Gravitation.
- **Electrodynamics**: ~20-25%. Current Electricity, Electrostatics, Magnetism.
- **Optics**: ~10%. Ray and Wave Optics.
- **Modern Physics**: ~10-12%. Atoms, Nuclei, Dual Nature of Matter.
- **Heat and Thermodynamics**: ~8-10%.

### Chemistry (25% Weightage - 180 Marks)
- **Organic Chemistry**: ~35%. General Organic Chemistry (GOC), Hydrocarbons, Aldehydes, Ketones, and Carboxylic Acids.
- **Physical Chemistry**: ~30%. Equilibrium, Thermodynamics, Solutions, Chemical Kinetics.
- **Inorganic Chemistry**: ~30%. Chemical Bonding, P-block elements, Coordination Compounds.

## Preparation Strategy for English Medium
- **NCERT Textbooks**: The primary source for all three subjects.
- **Standard Reference Books**: HC Verma (Physics), OP Tandon (Chemistry), Trueman's (Biology).
- **Previous Year Questions (PYQs)**: Solving at least the last 10 years' papers.
- **Mock Tests**: Regular practice to manage time and accuracy.
