# NEET 2026 Physics Crash Course

## Key Topics and Formulas

### 1. Mechanics

#### Laws of Motion
*   **Newton's First Law:** An object at rest stays at rest and an object in motion stays in motion with the same speed and in the same direction unless acted upon by an unbalanced force.
*   **Newton's Second Law:** The rate of change of momentum of an object is directly proportional to the applied unbalanced force in the direction of the force. `F = ma`
    *   `F`: Force
    *   `m`: Mass
    *   `a`: Acceleration
*   **Newton's Third Law:** For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction.

#### Work, Energy and Power
*   **Work:** `W = F . d = Fd cosθ`
    *   `W`: Work
    *   `F`: Force
    *   `d`: Displacement
*   **Kinetic Energy:** `KE = 1/2 mv²`
*   **Potential Energy:** `PE = mgh`
*   **Power:** `P = W/t = Fv`

#### Gravitation
*   **Newton's Law of Gravitation:** `F = Gm₁m₂/r²`
    *   `G`: Gravitational Constant
    *   `m₁, m₂`: Masses
    *   `r`: Distance between masses
*   **Acceleration due to gravity:** `g = GM/R²`

### 2. Electrodynamics

#### Current Electricity
*   **Ohm's Law:** `V = IR`
    *   `V`: Potential Difference
    *   `I`: Current
    *   `R`: Resistance
*   **Resistivity:** `ρ = RA/L`
*   **Resistors in Series:** `R_eq = R₁ + R₂ + ...`
*   **Resistors in Parallel:** `1/R_eq = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ + ...`

#### Magnetism
*   **Biot-Savart Law:** `dB = (μ₀/4π) (Idl sinθ / r²)`
*   **Ampere's Circuital Law:** `∮ B . dl = μ₀I_enc`
*   **Lorentz Force:** `F = q(E + v x B)`

### 3. Optics

#### Refraction and Dispersion
*   **Snell's Law:** `n₁ sinθ₁ = n₂ sinθ₂`
*   **Lens Formula:** `1/f = 1/v - 1/u`
*   **Mirror Formula:** `1/f = 1/v + 1/u`

### 4. Modern Physics

#### Atoms and Nuclei
*   **Bohr's Atomic Model:** Energy levels, radii.
*   **Radioactivity:** Half-life, Mean life.
*   **Mass-Energy Equivalence:** `E = mc²`

## Sample Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

**1. If a force of 10 N acts on an object and displaces it by 2 m, what is the work done?**
   a) 5 J
   b) 10 J
   c) 20 J
   d) 0 J

   **Answer:** c) 20 J (W = Fd = 10 N * 2 m = 20 J)

**2. According to Ohm's Law, what is the relationship between potential difference (V) and current (I)?**
   a) V = I/R
   b) I = VR
   c) V = IR
   d) R = VI

   **Answer:** c) V = IR

**3. Where is the value of acceleration due to gravity highest?**
   a) Equator
   b) Poles
   c) Center of the Earth
   d) Surface of the Moon

   **Answer:** b) Poles

**4. If the focal length of a lens is 20 cm, what is its power?**
   a) +5 D
   b) -5 D
   c) +0.05 D
   d) -0.05 D

   **Answer:** a) +5 D (P = 1/f = 1/0.20 m = 5 D)

**5. What is the half-life of a radioactive substance?**
   a) The time taken for a substance to completely decay
   b) The time taken for half of the atoms of a substance to decay
   c) The total number of atoms in a substance
   d) The average life of a substance

   **Answer:** b) The time taken for half of the atoms of a substance to decay
