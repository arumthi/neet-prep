# NEET 2026 Chemistry Crash Course

## Key Topics and Reactions

### 1. Organic Chemistry

#### General Organic Chemistry (GOC)
*   **Electronic Displacement Effects:** Inductive effect, Mesomeric effect, Hyperconjugation.
*   **Types of Organic Reactions:** Substitution, Addition, Elimination, Rearrangement.
*   **Acidity and Basicity:** Carboxylic acids, Alcohols, Amines.

#### Hydrocarbons
*   **Alkanes:** Preparation methods, Reactions (Halogenation).
*   **Alkenes:** Preparation methods, Reactions (Markovnikov's rule, Ozonolysis).
*   **Alkynes:** Preparation methods, Reactions.
*   **Benzene:** Aromaticity, Electrophilic Substitution Reactions - Nitration, Halogenation, Friedel-Crafts reactions.

### 2. Physical Chemistry

#### Equilibrium
*   **Chemical Equilibrium:** Equilibrium constant `K_c`, `K_p`.
*   **Le Chatelier's Principle:** Effect of concentration, pressure, temperature on equilibrium.
*   **Ionic Equilibrium:** Acids and Bases, pH calculations, Buffer solutions, Solubility product.

#### Thermodynamics
*   **Laws of Thermodynamics:** First Law `ΔU = Q + W`, Second Law `ΔS_total ≥ 0`.
*   **Enthalpy:** `ΔH`, Enthalpy of formation, Bond enthalpy.
*   **Entropy:** `ΔS`.
*   **Gibbs Free Energy:** `ΔG = ΔH - TΔS`, Spontaneous processes.

#### Solutions
*   **Concentration Units:** Molarity, Molality, Mole fraction.
*   **Raoult's Law:** Lowering of vapor pressure.
*   **Colligative Properties:** Lowering of vapor pressure, Elevation in boiling point, Depression in freezing point, Osmotic pressure.

### 3. Inorganic Chemistry

#### Chemical Bonding and Molecular Structure
*   **Ionic Bond:** Lattice energy.
*   **Covalent Bond:** Lewis structure, VSEPR theory, Hybridization, Molecular Orbital Theory (MOT).
*   **Hydrogen Bonding.**

#### p-block elements
*   **Group 13 to 18:** General properties, important compounds (Ammonia, Sulfuric acid, Nitric acid, Halogens).

#### Coordination Compounds
*   **Nomenclature of Coordination Compounds.**
*   **Werner's Theory.**
*   **Ligands:** Types.
*   **VBT and CFT (Valence Bond Theory and Crystal Field Theory):** Bonding and magnetic properties of coordination compounds.

## Sample Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

**1. Which of the following undergoes electrophilic substitution reaction?**
   a) Alkane
   b) Alkene
   c) Benzene
   d) Alkyne

   **Answer:** c) Benzene

**2. If the pH of a solution is 7, it is _______.**
   a) Acidic
   b) Basic
   c) Neutral
   d) Buffer solution

   **Answer:** c) Neutral

**3. The first law of thermodynamics refers to what?**
   a) Law of conservation of energy
   b) Increase in entropy
   c) Gibbs free energy
   d) Work and heat

   **Answer:** a) Law of conservation of energy

**4. `1/f = 1/v - 1/u` is the formula for which equation?**
   a) Mirror formula
   b) Lens formula
   c) Ohm's law
   d) Raoult's law

   **Answer:** b) Lens formula

**5. What is the oxidation state of Cobalt in the coordination compound [Co(NH₃)₆]³⁺?**
   a) +1
   b) +2
   c) +3
   d) 0

   **Answer:** c) +3
